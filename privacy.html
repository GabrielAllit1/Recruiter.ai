Recruiter.ai Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Recruiter.ai is a browser extension designed to help job seekers analyze resumes and evaluate job opportunities.

Information Processed

Recruiter.ai may process:

  • Resume information voluntarily provided by the user
  • Public professional profile information extracted from pages selected by the user
  • Public job posting information analyzed by the extension

Data Processing

Resume parsing, candidate profile generation, match scoring, and ghost-job analysis are performed locally in the user's browser whenever possible.

Recruiter.ai does not sell user data.

Recruiter.ai does not maintain user accounts.

Recruiter.ai does not store resumes on Recruiter.ai servers.

Third-Party Sources

Recruiter.ai may retrieve publicly available job listing information from third-party job sources to provide job matching and analysis functionality.

User Control

Users control what information is analyzed and may clear locally stored data at any time by removing extension storage or uninstalling the extension.
